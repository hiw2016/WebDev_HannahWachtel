About Me

Hi! I’m Hannah. Ever since I was little, I’ve found joy in the kitchen, whether that was baking, cooking, or helping with the dishes. Baking has always felt like magic to me, and for as long as I can remember, it’s been my favorite way to show love and creativity.

Still, like a lot of people, I wasn’t sure if I could turn that passion into something real. I baked on the side, baking for fun and occasionally gifting treats to neighbors. I started taking commissions for birthday cakes, cupcakes for baby showers, and more. What started as a few orders here and there quickly grew into something so much more.

I’ve poured my heart into every creation, and I’m so grateful for the support and love I’ve received along the way. When I’m not in the kitchen, you can probably find me using my creativity in other ways, either in graphic design or traditional art. Thank you for being here. I can’t wait to bake for you!
